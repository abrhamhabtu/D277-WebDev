Welcome to the Mile High City

Denver, the capital of Colorado, sits exactly one mile above sea level, offering a unique blend of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure. As a major hub of the Front Range Urban Corridor, Denver combines historic charm with modern innovation, making it one of America's fastest-growing and most vibrant cities.

City Statistics

  • Population: 716,234
  • Founded: 1861
  • Region: Central Colorado, Front Range Urban Corridor
  • Classification: Urban Metropolitan Center
  • Average Income: $85,853/year (15% above state average)

Quick Facts

  • Nickname: The Mile High City (5,280 ft elevation)
  • Area: 154.9 square miles
  • Climate: 300+ days of sunshine annually
  • Airport: Denver International (Largest airport by land area in U.S.)
