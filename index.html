Welcome To The Centennial State

Colorado, nicknamed the Centennial State because it achieved statehood in 1876—a century after the United States was founded, is known for its stunning Rocky Mountains, vibrant cities, and outdoor adventures. From the Capital Denver to the scenic beauty of Boulder and Colorado Springs, there's always something to discover.

Top 3 Most Visited Attractions

  1. Rocky Mountain National Park (Over 5 million visitors annually)
  2. Garden of the Gods (Over 3.4 million visitors annually)
  3. Red Rocks Amphitheatre (Over 1.65 million visitors annually)
